about me

Hi! I’m Leigh.

I am a web developer with a strong background in the humanities and social sciences. I want to use my experiences studying human societies in the past and present to bring unique solutions and ethical perspectives into my work.

I began studying web design at HackCville Launch in June 2018, where I learned HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I continued to advance my skills and started learning React in an online course after completeing Launch.

I am now open to full-time opportunities.
